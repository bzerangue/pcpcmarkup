Outreach & Help

Ways to help & enrich our community.

Becoming a Believer - God invites His children into relationship with Him and into the newness of life found in Jesus Christ.

Biblical Counseling - We meet the emotional and spiritual needs of our church body and partner with ministries to provide biblical counseling.

Deaf & Hard of Hearing - Our desire is to bring the corporate worship experience to those with hearing loss.

Financial Assistance - We offer financial support and guidance, counseling, and spiritual mentoring to individuals and families.

Funerals - Request our facilities for a funeral and a pastor to conduct a memorial service.

Hospital & Home Visits- Call on our church body for support and get further assistance from a pastor or minister.
